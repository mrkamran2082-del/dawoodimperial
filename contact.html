Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

All our products come with certificates of authenticity. We source directly from authorized distributors and brands. Each item includes unique serial numbers and authentication codes that can be verified.

We offer free shipping on orders above PKR 5,000. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for an additional fee with 1-2 day delivery.

We offer a 7-day return policy for unopened products in original packaging. Items must be in resalable condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders. We also accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallet payments (JazzCash, Easypaisa).

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in real-time through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support.
